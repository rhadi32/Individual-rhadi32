COVID-19 Vaccinations in the United States, Jurisdiction

Time Period, Geography, and/or Scope Covered:
In the United States at national and jurisdiction level. Data represents all vaccine partners including jurisdictional partner clinics, retail pharmacies, long-term care facilities, dialysis centers, Federal Emergency Management Agency and Health Resources and Services Administration partner sites, and federal entity facilities.
